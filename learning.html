1. What is Stock Trading?

Trading is buying and selling stocks to make a profit from price changes. Traders usually hold positions for shorter periods than investors.

2. Types of Trading

  • Intraday Trading: Buying and selling on the same day.
  • Swing Trading: Holding for a few days to weeks.
  • Delivery Trading: Holding for long term.

3. Basic Terminology

  • Support & Resistance
  • Volume
  • Open Interest (OI)
  • Stop Loss
  • Risk/Reward Ratio

4. Tips for Beginners

  • Start small and use a demo account.
  • Never trade based on tips or rumors.
  • Learn basic chart patterns and indicators.
  • Always use a stop-loss to protect capital.
